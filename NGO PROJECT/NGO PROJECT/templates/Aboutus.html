ABOUT US

We"Helping hands" are individuals who want selflessly lend a hand to the children in need. We offer support, kindness, and assistance, making a positive impact on the lives of those we help.
Whether it's volunteering, donating, or simply being there for someone, our helping hands wants to make the world a better place.

OUR VISION:

Our vision is to help poor children which can make a huge difference in their lives.This is a kind and compassionate act that helps provide them with basic necessities and opportunities for a better future. Giving back to those in need brings happiness and fulfillment to both the giver and the receiver.

OUR MISSION:

At Helping Hands, our mission is to make a positive impact in our community by providing support and assistance to those in need.
